Ferret is a visualization tool, designed to make artifacts of manipulation in tabular datasets salient.
The following datasets are examples of real datasets that are associated with papers that have been retracted due to concerns with the data.
Click any dataset below to open it in Ferret, or upload your own dataset to view it in Ferret.
